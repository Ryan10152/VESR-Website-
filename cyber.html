Industry and Role

The Industry my group chose was an Accounting Firm. Accounting Firms help people keep their finances organized and check for errors for fraud. They mostly handle legal cases that apply to accounting situations. My role within this Industry would be a Financial Database Analyst. They are responsible for interpreting and analyzing financial data to create reports and outlooks for the firm.

Possible Issues

Ethical Issue

A good example of an ethical issue within this industry would be a breach of confidentiality. Since we are dealing with people's personal information, employees have access to this information. The chance of someone sharing client information is possible.

Cybersecurity Issue

Data breaches are a possibility. Dealing with legal cases has a chance to possibly anger someone. And say maybe they could hire a hacker to access our system and steal critical information.

Cybersecurity Issue

An insecure cloud system or 3rd party application is a possible issue. Dealing with so many files and documents would be done through a cloud-based database; the chance of this cloud database having weak security or a server-wide breach is possible.

Cybersecurity Practices within the Industry

Some common Cybersecurity practices in accounting firms include data encryption, multi-factor authentication, risk assessments, and data backups.
